## ❓ FAQ

### Q: Where's the actual code?
**A**: Not written yet! This is the blueprint. Code begins Week 1 after Architecture Agent finalizes the design. By Week 2, you should have basic backend/frontend scaffolding running.

### Q: How long will this take?
**A**: MVP in 4 weeks (Weeks 1-4), Growth in 4 weeks (Weeks 5-8), Scaling in 4 weeks (Weeks 9-12). See IMPLEMENTATION_ROADMAP.md for weekly breakdown.

### Q: What's the budget?
**A**: Not specified here. You'll need:
- Cloud infrastructure (AWS): $500-2K/month (start small)
- Third-party APIs (NewsAPI, etc.): $100-500/month
- Legal/Compliance review: $5-10K upfront
- Team (if paid): Varies
- See your pitch deck & business plan for details.

### Q: Is there a financial advisor disclaimer I can use?
**A**: Yes! See COMPLIANCE_FRAMEWORK.md under "Required Disclaimers". Copy-paste the Primary Disclaimer onto every page.

### Q: Can we skip the legal stuff?
**A**: No. This is non-negotiable. See "Core Principles" in COMPLIANCE_FRAMEWORK.md. You will get sued or fined otherwise. Hire a lawyer.

### Q: What if we need to pivot?
**A**: This roadmap is flexible. It's a guide, not gospel. Review & adjust at weekly standups. Document changes in a "Decisions Log".

### Q: What should the team size be?
**A**: The 6 AI agents can replace 15-20 humans if configured right. Start with:
- You (Product)
- Haruto (Engineering)
- 6 AI Agents (parallel tracks)

If budget allows: Add 1-2 humans per phase.

### Q: How do I measure success?
**A**: Use success criteria in PROJECT_CHARTER.md and IMPLEMENTATION_ROADMAP.md. Track weekly.

### Q: Who do I contact for help?
**A**: See README.md "Contact & Communication" section. Start in Slack #general or ask Haruto.
