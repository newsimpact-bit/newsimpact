# MarketPulse AI - Project Charter

## 🎯 Vision
Create a trustworthy, legally compliant AI-powered platform that helps users understand how global news may influence financial markets in real-time.

---

## 👥 Founding Team

| Role | Founder | Responsibility |
|------|---------|-----------------|
| Co-Founder & Product Lead | You | Product vision, strategy, business development |
| Co-Founder & Technical Lead | Haruto | Architecture, core infrastructure, AI systems |

---

## 🏗️ Core Pillars

1. **Accuracy First** - All insights grounded in verifiable data
2. **Transparency Always** - Clear disclaimers, confidence levels, source attribution
3. **Legal Compliance** - Full adherence to financial regulations and copyright law
4. **User-Centric Design** - Intuitive, fast, reliable interface

---

## 📊 Product Tiers

### Free Tier
- Daily news summaries
- Limited asset impact insights
- Basic sentiment analysis
- 10-30 min delayed updates
- 50 queries/day

### Premium Tier ($9.99-$29.99/month)
- Real-time news & analysis
- Full Market Impact Engine
- Advanced filtering & search
- Deep-dive reports & scenario modeling
- Personalized watchlists
- Breaking news alerts
- Unlimited queries

### Enterprise Tier (Custom)
- API access for businesses
- White-label options
- Custom integrations
- Dedicated support

---

## 🎯 Success Metrics

- **Accuracy**: 85%+ correctness of market-impact predictions vs. actual outcomes
- **Compliance**: Zero regulatory violations or copyright strikes
- **User Growth**: 10K+ free users in Year 1
- **Retention**: 40%+ conversion from free to premium
- **Performance**: <2s page load times, real-time updates within 60 seconds

---

## ⏱️ Phased Rollout

**Phase 1 (Weeks 1-4)**: MVP with 5 major news sources, basic sentiment analysis
**Phase 2 (Weeks 5-8)**: Premium tier, advanced filtering, historical analogs
**Phase 3 (Weeks 9-12)**: API, enterprise features, advanced analytics
**Phase 4 (Q2+)**: Expansion to social sentiment, earnings transcripts, macro dashboards
